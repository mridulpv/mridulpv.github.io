1. Introduction

Welcome to QR Code Generator (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Information We Collect

We value your privacy — our App does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No registration or login is required.
  • We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
  • All data used for generating QR codes (such as text, links, or Wi‑Fi credentials) remains on your device only.
  • The App does not transmit any data to external servers.

3. Permissions Used

To provide core functionality, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used only to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • Storage / Media Access: Used to save generated QR codes to your device gallery.

These permissions are used strictly for their intended purpose and never for data collection.

4. How Your Data Is Used

All QR code data is processed locally on your device. We do not send, store, or analyze the contents of your QR codes anywhere online. You have full control over your generated and scanned data.

5. Internet Access

The App may use the internet only if you choose to open a website or link contained in a QR code. We do not use internet access for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

6. Data Security

All operations (QR creation, scanning, saving) are handled locally within your device. We never upload or store your QR code content on our servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted with an updated “Effective Date” above.
